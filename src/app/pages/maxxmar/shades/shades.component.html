Vertical Shades

The Vertical Shadings feature an innovative and unique design that combines the best features and functionality of horizontal, vertical, and sunscreen shades into a single luxurious shading product.
Unlike vertical blinds, Vertical Shadings allow you to raise the blinds like a roller shade while giving you a completely open view to the exterior. An excellent feature especially for homes with patio doors, garden doors, and French doors.
